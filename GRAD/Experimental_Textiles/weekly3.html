Process Notes

We began working on our Cricket Loom in class on September 7th, 2022. After my partner and I assembled the loom, we started setting the warp on the heddle. We also marked the middle of our heddle and where we wanted to begin and end our warp threads. After threading it through the slots, we cut off the yarn from the end pedestal and wrapped up the warn using the rachet. Once that was complete we started brining one strand through each hole, and then finally tying them to the pole with a surgens knot (for easy tightning-tune-ups later). At this point, our loom was ready for wefts but our class time had run out. Unfortunately, I am going to be gone this weekend working in Grand Junction so scheduling time with my partner may prove to be challenging.
