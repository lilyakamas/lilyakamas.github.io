Frequently asked question

How can do I use the application?

Use the sliders on the homepage to select your current mood, as well as your desired mood

Should I use this every day?

This application is intended for daily use, however, you can use it as frequently as you need to.

Should I use this on my phone or computer?

You can use this application on whichever platform you use most often. However, it is accessible on all devices!

How can I access the Analytics or Recommendations?

These will be updated by the authors soon!

What is this for?

The Mood Ring Application is designed at the University of Colorado, Boulder as a Senior Undergraduate Capstone project for Information Science. Author information is on the “About Us” Page
